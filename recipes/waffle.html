Waffle Recipe

Beautiful, fluffy waffles cooked to perfection everytime.

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose Flour
  • 1 Tbsp Sugar
  • 2 Tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 Tsp salt
  • 3 large Eggs, separated
  • 2 cups Milk
  • 1/4 cup Oil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat Waffle Maker
  2. Crack the eggs and seperate the yolk from the whites
  3. Whisk the whites from the eggs until it forms stiff peaks
  4. Whisk the egg yolks with milk, sugar and oil
  5. Fold in the salt, baking powder and flour
  6. Cook the waffles for 5-6 mins or until golden brown and crispy
